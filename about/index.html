Biography

I’m just a node in the graph of human relations, trying to understand my role in this world by questioning how it is that I got here. But I do it at a different physical scale.

I dare think about the work I do as art – as did others – though I am not really an art person (sigh) and surely not a great artist. But art is fuelled by curiosity, curiosity is the driver of science, and curious people seek beauty.
